Protein Structure Prediction Models

MODELLER

MODELLER is a popular software for homology or comparative modeling of protein three-dimensional structures. You provide an alignment of a sequence to be modeled with known related structures and MODELLER automatically calculates a model containing all non-hydrogen atoms.

Robetta

Robetta is a protein structure prediction service that uses a combination of homology modeling and ab initio methods. It can be used to predict the structure of proteins with or without known homologous structures.
