Welcome to ProSutra!

ProSutra is a powerful desktop application designed to streamline the process of protein structure prediction. It integrates two popular homology modeling tools, MODELLER and Robetta, providing a user-friendly interface to predict the 3D structure of a protein from its amino acid sequence.

In addition to prediction, ProSutra also offers a suite of validation tools to assess the quality of the generated models. This allows researchers to have more confidence in their predicted structures.

This application is the result of a collaborative effort by a team of dedicated developers, aiming to make protein structure prediction more accessible to the scientific community.
